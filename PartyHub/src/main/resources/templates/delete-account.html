How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the app and log in to your account.

2. Go to your account profile page.

3. Beneath the username, you will see a trash can icon.

4. Click on this icon to initiate the account deletion process.

5. Confirm the action and your account will be permanently deleted.

Ensure you have saved or transferred any important information or data you wish to keep before deleting the account, as this process is irreversible.

If you encounter any difficulties or have additional questions, please contact us for assistance.
